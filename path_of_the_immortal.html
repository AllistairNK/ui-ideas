Cultivation and Heavenly Tribulation Guide

1. Gathering Qi

Sit in meditative lotus position or locate glowing blue Spiritual Springs in the world to boost your passive cultivation rate enormously.

2. Beast Hunt & Alchemy

Move near Demonic beasts to strike them with your Flying Sword. Defeated beasts drop Cores; gather Spirit Grass too. Combine them in the Alchemy Cauldron to craft breakthrough pills!

3. Surviving Heavenly Tribulation

Once your Qi reaches maximum, acquire the necessary Pill, and trigger the breakthrough. Avoid the flashing red circles—lightning will strike them! Survive the storm to advance your Realm status.
